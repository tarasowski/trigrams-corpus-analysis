The red fox is a small to medium-sized mammal known for its distinctive red fur and bushy tail. Foxes are highly adaptable and can be found in many different environments including urban areas. The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog is a popular sentence used for typing practice because it includes all the letters of the English alphabet. Foxes are known for their agility, speed, and cunning nature. They have a varied diet that includes small mammals, birds, and fruits.
