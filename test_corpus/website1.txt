The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog. The quick brown fox is known for its agility and speed. Foxes are small to medium-sized omnivorous mammals belonging to several genera of the family Canidae. Foxes have a flattened skull, upright triangular ears, a pointed, slightly upturned snout, and a long bushy tail. Their habitat ranges from urban to rural settings. Quick and cunning, foxes have adapted well to human environments.
